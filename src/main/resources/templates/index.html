Student Monitoring System

Overview

Welcome to the AI-Based Day Student Monitoring System. This system helps in tracking student attendance at school gates and classrooms to ensure timely monitoring and effective communication with parents.

Morning Check-In

Monitor student check-in at the gate (06:00 AM - 09:59 AM).

Evening Check-Out

Track student departure from school (04:00 PM - 06:00 PM).

Classroom Attendance

Teachers can mark student attendance for each session.

Gate Attendance Reports

Generate detailed reports for gate attendance tracking, ensuring proper monitoring of students' arrival and departure.

Class Attendance Reports

Access classroom attendance data to review students' presence across different sessions, with daily, weekly, and monthly summaries.
